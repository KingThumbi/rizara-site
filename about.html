Our Mission & Vision

Rizara Meats is committed to building a transparent, sustainable, and scalable halal meat ecosystem in East Africa. Our mission is to provide ethically sourced, fully traceable goat and sheep meat for GCC and international markets.

Our vision is to become the leading East African supplier of high-quality halal protein, supporting farmers, investors, and buyers with a fully integrated supply chain.

Our Story

Rizara Meats was founded to bridge the gap between farmers and halal meat buyers, creating a transparent and reliable supply chain. We provide farmers with training, access to resources, and direct market channels for high-demand livestock such as goat and sheep.

Investors and partners can participate in a structured ecosystem that tracks every animal from farm to export-ready processing, ensuring full compliance with GCC and international halal standards.

Why Rizara Meats

Full Traceability

Every animal is tracked from farm to fork, ensuring halal integrity, transparency, and compliance with international standards.

Investor Friendly
