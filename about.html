Our Story

Rizara Meats bridges the gap between local farmers and global buyers, ensuring halal compliance, ethical livestock management, and sustainable meat supply chains. Our mission is to provide high-quality, traceable halal meat to GCC markets while empowering farmers and supporting donors & investors.

Our Vision

To be the leading halal meat supplier in East Africa, trusted by local and international buyers for quality, sustainability, and transparency.

Our Mission

  • Provide ethically sourced, high-quality halal meat.
  • Empower local farmers with training and market access.
  • Engage investors and donors in sustainable livestock development.
  • Ensure full traceability and halal compliance from farm to fork.

Why Choose Us?

  • Premium halal meat meeting GCC and local standards.
  • Ethically managed livestock farms and processing.
  • Direct connection between farmers, buyers, and investors.
  • Transparent, traceable supply chain from farm to fork.
