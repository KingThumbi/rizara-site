Our Story

Rizara Meats was founded to bridge the gap between local farmers and global buyers. We ensure halal compliance, ethical livestock management, and sustainable meat supply chains. Our mission is to provide high-quality, traceable halal meat to the GCC region while empowering local farmers and supporting donors and investors who care about ethical livestock production.

Our Vision

To be the leading halal meat supplier in East Africa, trusted by both local and international buyers for quality, sustainability, and transparency.

Our Mission

  • Provide ethically sourced, high-quality halal meat.
  • Empower local farmers through training and access to markets.
  • Engage investors and donors in sustainable livestock development.
  • Ensure full traceability and halal compliance from farm to fork.

Why Choose Us?

  • Premium halal meat meeting GCC and local standards.
  • Ethically managed livestock farms and processing.
  • Direct connection between farmers, buyers, and investors.
  • Transparent, traceable supply chain from farm to fork.
