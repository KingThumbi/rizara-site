About Rizara Meats

Rizara Meats is building a trusted halal meat supply ecosystem that connects Kenyan farmers to local and international markets through ethical sourcing, quality processing, and full traceability.

Our Story

Rizara Meats was founded to solve a growing gap between livestock producers and reliable meat markets. While demand for halal meat continues to rise across Kenya and the GCC region, many farmers lack access to structured buyers, fair pricing, and export-ready supply chains.

By aggregating livestock, enforcing quality standards, and coordinating processing and logistics, Rizara creates value for farmers while delivering consistent, high-quality halal meat to buyers.

Our Mission

To supply ethical, halal-certified livestock and meat products while empowering farmers through fair markets, transparency, and long-term partnerships.

Our Vision

To become a leading halal meat supply platform from East Africa to global markets, driven by trust, traceability, and sustainability.

How We Operate

Rizara Meats operates an integrated supply chain model that ensures quality and accountability at every stage.

Farmer Onboarding

We work with livestock keepers across Kenya, verifying animal health, breed suitability, and production practices.

Aggregation

Animals are aggregated at controlled hubs where welfare, feeding, and health checks are maintained.

Halal Processing

Slaughter and processing follow strict halal and veterinary standards under qualified supervision.

Distribution

Products are delivered locally or prepared for export using cold-chain logistics.

Halal, Quality & Compliance

Compliance is central to everything we do. Our processes align with Islamic halal requirements, food safety regulations, and buyer specifications.

  • Halal-certified slaughter procedures
  • Veterinary inspection and health clearance
  • Export-grade hygiene and handling standards
  • End-to-end livestock and product traceability

Our Ecosystem

Rizara Meats collaborates with a growing ecosystem of stakeholders to ensure quality, scale, and sustainability.

Farmers & Aggregators

Veterinary & Feed Experts

Buyers & Partners
