Place an Order

Rizara Meats supplies halal livestock and meat products to local buyers, institutions, and international markets. Submit your order request below and our team will guide you through fulfillment.

Who Can Order

Local Buyers

Restaurants, butcheries, retailers, and households seeking reliable halal meat supply within Kenya.

Institutions

Hotels, hospitals, schools, NGOs, and catering companies requiring consistent bulk supply.

Export Buyers

GCC and international buyers sourcing export-grade halal meat with documentation and traceability.

Available Products

  • Live livestock (goats, sheep, cattle)
  • Fresh halal meat (whole carcass or cuts)
  • Bulk institutional supply
  • Export-prepared chilled or frozen meat

How Ordering Works

1. Submit Request

Share your order details including quantity, product type, and delivery location.

2. Confirmation

Our team confirms availability, pricing, and timelines based on your requirements.

3. Processing

Livestock is aggregated, processed under halal standards, and prepared for delivery.

4. Delivery

Orders are delivered locally or prepared for export with cold-chain logistics.
