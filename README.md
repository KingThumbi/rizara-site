# Rizara Meats Website

Official front-end website for **Rizara Meats**, a halal meat supplier connecting local farmers with local and GCC buyers. This site showcases products, company story, and provides a buyer kiosk for placing orders.

---

## **Folder Structure**

rizara-site/
├── index.html # Homepage
├── about.html # About / company info
├── packages.html # Products / meat offerings
├── contact.html # Contact page & form
├── orders.html # Buyer order kiosk
├── assets/
│ ├── css/
│ │ └── style.css # Branding and styling
│ └── images/ # All images referenced in the site
├── README.md
└── .gitignore

## **Features**

- Fully responsive layout for desktop and mobile
- Hero section with high-quality livestock imagery
- About section highlighting company story, mission, and vision
- Products page showcasing meat offerings and live livestock
- Contact page with phone, email, and postal address (P.O BOX 25399-00100, GPO Nairobi)
- Buyer kiosk page (`orders.html`) to place orders online
- Donors & investors section
- Consistent branding with Jungle Green and Gold colors
- Accessible and clean design

---

## **Deployment Instructions**

1. Upload the **entire `rizara-site` folder** to your hosting root (e.g., `/public_html`).
2. Ensure the **`assets/css/style.css`** and **`assets/images/`** folder are intact.
3. Deploy via **cPanel Git** or FTP.
4. Open the site URL in a browser; all pages are linked and functional.
