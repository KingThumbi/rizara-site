Our Products & Supply Packages

Rizara Meats offers a wide range of halal livestock and meat supply solutions tailored for households, institutions, and international buyers. All products are ethically sourced, halal-certified, and fully traceable.

Live Livestock Supply

We aggregate livestock from vetted farmers across Kenya, ensuring animal health, welfare, and compliance with halal standards.

Goats

Ideal for local consumption and export markets. Raised in semi-arid regions with strong disease management.

Sheep

High-quality breeds suitable for premium meat production and seasonal demand peaks.

Cattle

Carefully selected cattle for institutional buyers and bulk processing requirements.

Processed Halal Meat

Our meat is processed under strict halal supervision and prepared for both domestic and international distribution.

Fresh Meat

Whole carcasses or customized cuts delivered fresh to butcheries, restaurants, and institutions.

Bulk Institutional Supply

Reliable, recurring supply for hotels, hospitals, schools, and NGOs with consistent quality.

Export-Ready Meat

Chilled or frozen meat prepared for GCC and international markets with required documentation.
